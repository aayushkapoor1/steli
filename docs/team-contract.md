# Team Contract

## Roles

- **Aayush** is the team lead and is responsible for coordinating the project.
- **Sophie** leads frontend development.
- **Alex** leads backend development.
- **Ben** leads design.
- **Daniel** leads integration and end-to-end testing of the application.
- **Alyssa** leads the design and maintenance of the database.

## Communication

- The team will use an SMS group chat for all project-related communication.
- The team will meet weekly on **Tuesdays at 3:30 PM** in **Engineering 7**.

## Meetings

- All members are expected to attend meetings or notify the team in advance if they anticipate being absent. Absences should be rare.
- All members will come to meetings prepared by:
  - reading any assigned material (as much as possible), and
  - bringing ideas related to the tasks and decisions to be made.
- Members will remain engaged during meetings and will not work on other assignments.
- Decisions may be made during meetings even if some members are absent.
- Each member will take turns speaking and listening, and active listening will be practiced during all group discussions.
- **Daniel** will post the meeting agenda in the group chat each Monday.
- **Aayush** will take notes during all meetings.

## Conflict Resolution

- In the event of a conflict, both parties will discuss their options with the group.
- If necessary, the group will resolve the issue through a democratic vote.
